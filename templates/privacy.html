Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

AI Life Coach is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal thoughts, goals, and life experiences should remain private and under your control.

What Information We Collect

  • Conversation Data: Your messages and our AI responses are stored locally on your device
  • Goals and Life Events: Personal goals and life events you choose to share
  • Usage Patterns: Basic analytics to improve your coaching experience
  • Technical Data: IP address for rate limiting and security purposes

How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored in a JSON file on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We don't automatically sync your data to cloud services
  • Full Control: You can export, backup, or delete your data at any time

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Your messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate AI responses
  • No Persistent Storage: OpenAI doesn't store your conversations permanently
  • Anonymized: No personally identifiable information is sent with requests

Your Rights

  • Data Export: Download all your data in JSON format
  • Data Deletion: Delete all stored data permanently
  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies or tracking scripts
  • Transparency: Full access to how your data is processed

Security Measures

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Input validation and sanitization
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally until you choose to delete it. We recommend regular backups if you want to preserve your coaching history.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the application interface.
